Max Temperature


As expected, the weather becomes significanlty warmer as one approaches the equator. More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmner this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
